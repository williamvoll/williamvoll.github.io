SEO

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the attempt to increase traffic to your website by ranking near the top on search engine result pages. For example, a website that sells cell phone cases would want to appear in the top search results when a potential customer searches for "cell phone case" in their search engine of choice.

There are multiple factors that affect SEO including site structure, content, keyword competition, relevant sites that link to your site, if site is mobile friendly, and site load time.

Search engines win when they match users with the right website. Websites win when they rank high for the correct keywords.

SEO is always changing. It requires constant research to stay up with the latest rules and tactics (which I like to do).
